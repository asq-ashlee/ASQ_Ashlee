ASQ Ashlee is a question-led platform exploring how AI changes the way we think, work, and live.

The brand exists at the intersection of:

* curiosity
* clarity
* systems thinking
* real life application

It is built on the belief that:
The future is not just about using AI — it is about learning how to think with it.

Core traits of the brand:

* thoughtful, not reactive
* intelligent, not technical
* warm, not performative
* exploratory, not authoritative

The visual identity reflects a duality:

* human + system
* intuition + structure
* question + answer

Color system:

* pink/red → human voice, energy, perspective (#ff3366)
* cyan/teal → systems, logic, AI, structure (#00e2ea / #00ffff)
* soft blue background → calm, approachable thinking space (#d6fafc)

Typography:

* clean, modern sans-serif (Garet or similar)

The tone of the brand is:

* grounded
* curious
* slightly editorial
* quietly confident

ASQ Ashlee is not positioned as an expert platform.

It is a space for:

* asking better questions
* making sense of complexity
* exploring ideas in a way that feels real and usable

The identity should always feel like:
A thinking space you trust.
