## Writing as Ashlee

### Core Principles

* sound human, not corporate
* write how it would be said out loud
* prioritize clarity over cleverness

---

### Tone

* curious, not authoritative
* grounded, not overconfident
* structured, but natural

---

### Style

* short paragraphs
* clear structure
* minimal fluff
* conversational flow

---

### Avoid

* jargon
* over-polished language
* trying to sound impressive
* unnecessary complexity

---

### Goal

Make ideas:

* easier to understand
* easier to think about
* easier to act on

Not:

* more complicated
* more technical
* more abstract
