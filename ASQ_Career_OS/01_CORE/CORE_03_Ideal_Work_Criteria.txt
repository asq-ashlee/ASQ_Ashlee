## Ideal Work Criteria

### Must Have

* flexible schedule
* autonomy in how work is done
* outcome-based expectations
* intellectually engaging problems

---

### Strong Preference

* AI / systems / workflow related
* cross-functional work
* ability to improve how things operate
* real-world application (not theoretical)

---

### Nice to Have

* remote or hybrid
* exposure to AI-forward teams
* opportunity to build something new

---

### Avoid

* rigid 9–5 presence roles
* repetitive execution work
* heavy bureaucracy
* environments resistant to change
* unclear ownership or impact

---

### Opportunity Types

* Core fit (ideal alignment)
* Low hanging fruit (fast income)
* Strategic (long-term value)
* Experimental (interesting but uncertain)
* Skip
