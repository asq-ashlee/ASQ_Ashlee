# ASQ Career OS — CONTEXT

Last updated: 2026-05-20
Status: active

## Purpose

ASQ Career OS is an AI-assisted career evaluation and operational methodology focused on helping people navigate modern work with greater clarity.

This workspace exists to:
- evaluate opportunities
- map workflows
- identify transferable skills
- reduce career noise
- analyze operational fit
- support sustainable career decisions

The system prioritizes:
- human energy
- operational fit
- long-term sustainability
- systems alignment
- realistic opportunity evaluation

---

## Core Activities

- role evaluations
- resume positioning
- transferable skill mapping
- operational analysis
- workflow assessment
- opportunity scoring
- AI-native career strategy

---

## Evaluation Philosophy

Roles should be evaluated based on:
- operational fit
- workflow alignment
- energy sustainability
- strategic value
- learning potential
- compensation
- AI exposure
- future scalability

Avoid:
- prestige-driven thinking
- hype-driven opportunities
- title obsession
- unsustainable workflows

---

## GPT Philosophy

GPTs are:
- interface layers
- workflow tools
- reasoning environments

NOT:
- the methodology itself
- persistent operational memory
- autonomous authority systems

The methodology lives in:
- frameworks
- source docs
- operational logic
- evaluation structures
