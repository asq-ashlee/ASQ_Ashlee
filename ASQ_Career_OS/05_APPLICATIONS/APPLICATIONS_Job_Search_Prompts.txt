## Daily Job Lead Scout Prompt

Find me 10 job leads using Career OS.

Separate results into:

1. Verified Postings
- Only include roles with a direct, specific job posting link.

2. Leads to Verify
- Include roles that look promising but need manual link verification.

For any lead without a verified direct posting link, give me the exact Google search query to find it.

Focus on:
- AI / Systems
- Marketing / Lifecycle
- Marketing Operations
- Operations / Account / Client Strategy
- Open / Adjacent

For each result, include:
- Role
- Company
- Link or “needs verification”
- Suggested search query, if needed
- Remote/location
- Salary, if listed
- Role Mode
- First-pass Opportunity Type
- First-pass Fit Estimate
- Why it may fit
- Concern
- Recommendation: evaluate now / save / skip
