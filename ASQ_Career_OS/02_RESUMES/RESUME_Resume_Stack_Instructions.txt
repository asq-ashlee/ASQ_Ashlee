## General Resume Rules

- Keep language simple and human.
- Avoid jargon unless the job description uses it.
- Prioritize clarity over cleverness.
- Make the work sound real, not inflated.
- Do not make Ashlee sound like a software engineer.
- Do not flatten her into a generic marketer.
- Position her as someone who makes messy systems easier to understand, improve, and scale.

## Tailoring Rules

For each job:
1. Start with the Core resume.
2. Identify the role mode:
   - AI / Systems
   - Marketing / Lifecycle
   - Operations / Account / Client Strategy
   - Event / Experiential
   - Open / Adjacent
3. Pull supporting proof from the most relevant resume and case studies.
4. Keep the same underlying truth, but shift emphasis.
5. Never create claims that are not supported by the source files.
