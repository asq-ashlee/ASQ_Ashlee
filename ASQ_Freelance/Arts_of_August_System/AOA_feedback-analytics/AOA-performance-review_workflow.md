# AOA Performance Review Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow reviews operational performance across:
- artwork
- products
- content
- sales channels
- audience engagement

---

## Review Areas

Evaluate:

- best-performing artwork themes
- strongest emotional resonance
- strongest sales channels
- strongest content hooks
- strongest product formats
- workflow bottlenecks
- operational friction

---

## Workflow Goals

Identify:
- repeatable successes
- workflow inefficiencies
- audience patterns
- future opportunities
- operational improvements

---

## Important Constraints

Avoid:
- over-optimizing for algorithms
- flattening artistic identity
- chasing trends aggressively
- reducing work to metrics alone

Preserve:
- artistic integrity
- emotional authenticity
- long-term sustainability
- human-centered operations
