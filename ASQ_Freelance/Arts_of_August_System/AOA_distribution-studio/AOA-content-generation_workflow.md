# AOA Content Generation Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow creates audience-facing communication assets for Arts of August.

---

## Read First

Review:

- CLAUDE.md
- asq-ashlee_context.md
- asq-freelance_context.md
- arts-of-august_context.md
- AOA_distribution-studio_context.md
- AOA_source-intelligence/
- linked artwork metadata
- linked product information

---

## Workflow Goals

Generate:
- Instagram captions
- Pinterest copy
- email concepts
- launch storytelling
- product highlights
- content hooks

---

## Required Content Fields

Each content item should contain:

- Platform
- Content Type
- Hook
- Caption/Copy
- Workflow Status
- Scheduled Date
- Linked Artwork
- Linked Product

---

## Workflow Status Definitions

Idea
- content concept only

Draft
- initial copy created

Ready for Review
- awaiting human review

Scheduled
- assigned publication timing

Posted
- publicly distributed

Archived
- inactive or retired

---

## Platform Definitions

Instagram
- emotional storytelling
- visual warmth
- human tone

Pinterest
- discovery-oriented
- aspirational
- home/lifestyle positioning

Email
- relationship building
- launches
- collector communication

Website
- evergreen storytelling
- collection positioning

---

## Important Constraints

Avoid:
- repetitive caption structures
- generic AI phrasing
- excessive hashtags
- corporate marketing tone
- exaggerated emotional language

Preserve:
- emotional authenticity
- calm warmth
- visual specificity
- artistic integrity

---

## Human Review Required

Human review required before:
- scheduling
- publishing
- email distribution
