FILE 01 — BRAND FOUNDATION

Arts of August is a body of work centered on quiet moments, coastal light, and place-based observation.

The work does not describe scenes—it notices them.

It captures:

* the hush before the day begins
* the glow after it ends
* the in-between states where light, color, and memory meet

The art is grounded in real places (Maine, coastal environments), but expressed through atmosphere rather than detail.

The goal is not storytelling.

The goal is:
to create a pause
