FILE 04 — LISTING SYSTEM

Structure

1. Immersive opening (brand voice)
2. Scene + atmosphere
3. Artist context
4. Product details
5. Palette & mood
6. Production & shipping
7. About the artist

Opening Paragraph Rules

* Start with the moment or condition
* Avoid: “This painting features…”
* Focus on light, stillness, time of day

Product Section Rules

* Clear, structured, repeatable
* No voice styling needed

Palette & Mood

* Reinforces emotional tone
* Can use light emotional language (controlled)

Separation Rule (critical)

* Description = brand expression
* Tags = SEO

Never mix them

## Layered Listing Construction

Arts of August listings combine information from multiple operational systems.

Examples:
- Product specifications → Printful or artist records
- Shipping logic → Etsy shipping profiles or Printful systems
- Emotional positioning → AI + human review
- Product imagery → artist photography, Printful mockups, Canva assets

AI may assist with:
- listing structure
- emotional storytelling
- product positioning
- title exploration
- readability improvements

AI should not invent operational product details that belong to authoritative systems.
