FILE 08 — AI GENERATION RULES

When generating a listing:

Step 1 — Title

* Create emotionally grounded title anchor
* Add SEO clarifier

Step 2 — Description

* Write immersive opening
* Describe atmosphere
* Add structured product section

Step 3 — Tags

* Use literal SEO phrases
* No poetic language

Guardrails

If output includes:

* perfect for your home
* beautiful artwork
* adds charm

It is incorrect and must be rewritten

## Operational Grounding Rules

Outputs should remain:
- operationally realistic
- structurally reusable
- emotionally grounded
- aligned with actual workflow stages

Do not optimize outputs for:
- impressiveness
- excessive creativity
- exaggerated emotional tone
- over-engineered operational suggestions

The system should prioritize:
- clarity
- realism
- usability
- interpretability
- human reviewability

The goal is:
useful operational intelligence, not maximal generation.
