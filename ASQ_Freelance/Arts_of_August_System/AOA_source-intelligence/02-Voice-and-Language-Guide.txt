FILE 02 — VOICE & LANGUAGE

Core Voice

* Observational, not declarative
* Atmospheric, not narrative
* Grounded, not abstract
* Controlled, never loud

The voice stays close to the scene.

Expression Rules

Always:

* Anchor to light, time, place, or condition
* Describe environment before object
* Let emotion emerge indirectly

Never:

* Explain meaning
* Tell a story
* Over-describe
* Use trend-based or décor language

Emotional Handling

* Do NOT name emotions directly in titles
* In descriptions, emotion may appear lightly (e.g., calm, nostalgia), but should remain restrained

Emotion should come from:

* light
* stillness
* timing

Language Patterns

Use:

* dawn, afternoon, evening
* glow, drift, hush, stillness
* color as tone (blush, verdigris, saffron, violet)

* perfect for your home
* beautiful wall art
