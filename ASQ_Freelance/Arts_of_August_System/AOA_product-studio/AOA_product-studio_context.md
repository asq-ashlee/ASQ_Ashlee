## Purpose

The Product Studio is responsible for transforming artwork into operational product systems.

This workspace manages:
- product generation
- print formats
- pricing
- fulfillment workflows
- sales channels
- mockups
- marketplace preparation
- listing readiness

## Product Philosophy

Products should:
- preserve artistic integrity
- feel visually cohesive
- remain operationally manageable
- support scalable workflows
- align with Arts of August brand tone

---

## Operational Distinctions

This workspace separates:

### Product Type
What the object IS.

Examples:
- Original Painting
- Framed Print
- Canvas Print
- Greeting Card
- Mini Print

### Fulfillment Method
How the customer receives the product.

Examples:
- Printful
- Manual Shipping
- Local Pickup
- Gallery Fulfillment

### Sales Channels
Where the product is sold.

Examples:
- Etsy
- Website
- Gallery
- Art Show
- Market
- Instagram
- Wholesale
