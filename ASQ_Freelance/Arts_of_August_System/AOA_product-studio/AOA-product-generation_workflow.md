# AOA Product Generation Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow expands a completed artwork into operational products.

---

## Read First

Review:

- CLAUDE.md
- asq-ashlee_context.md
- asq-freelance_context.md
- arts-of-august_context.md
- AOA_product-studio_context.md
- AOA_source-intelligence/
- linked artwork metadata

---

## Workflow Goals

Generate:
- logical print products
- marketplace-ready concepts
- fulfillment recommendations
- sales channel recommendations
- mockup recommendations

---

## Product Development Rules

Products should:
- match artwork mood
- support likely buyer environments
- remain operationally manageable
- fit Arts of August aesthetic

---

## Required Product Outputs

Each product should contain:

- Product Name
- Product Type
- Price
- Workflow Status
- Fulfillment Method
- Sales Channels
- Source Artwork
- Mockup Assets
- Marketplace Links

---

## Workflow Status Definitions

Idea
- product concept exists only

Draft
- operational product entry created

Mockup Needed
- visual assets incomplete

Ready for Listing
- ready for marketplace publication

Listed
- publicly available

Archived
- inactive or retired

---

## Human Review Required

Human review required before:
- pricing publication
- Etsy publication
- final product activation
