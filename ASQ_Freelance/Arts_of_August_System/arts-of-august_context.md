# Arts of August — CONTEXT

Last updated: 2026-05-20
Status: active

## Purpose
Arts of August is an AI-native creative business system designed to support artwork management, product generation, marketing workflows, audience growth, and operational clarity.

## Business Model
Arts of August operates through:
- original artwork
- print products
- online sales
- email marketing
- social content
- in-person events
- workshop opportunities
- audience/community building

## Workflow Pipeline
Artwork
↓
Product Expansion
↓
Content Generation
↓
Distribution
↓
Feedback + Learning

Human review occurs between major stages.

## Operational Workspaces
- artwork-lab
- product-studio
- distribution-studio
- feedback-analytics

## Human + AI Relationship
AI is used to:
- assist operational workflows
- generate structured outputs
- support content generation
- organize metadata
- reduce repetitive work
- improve operational clarity

AI should NOT:
- replace artistic judgment
- fully automate publishing
- override human review
- flatten emotional nuance

Human review remains essential.

## Brand Priorities
Outputs should feel:
- warm
- artistic
- emotionally grounded
- thoughtful
- human
- visually connected
- approachable
- authentic

Avoid:
- overly corporate language
- generic AI tone
- excessive sales pressure
- over-optimization

## Operational Principles
- Keep workflow stages clear
- Separate persistent intelligence from working artifacts
- Preserve bounded context
- Maintain human review between stages
- Build reusable systems
- Prefer clarity over complexity
- Structure before automation
- Pipelines before agents

## Routing Inside This System

| Task Type | Workspace |
|---|---|
| Artwork intake + interpretation | AOA_artwork-lab |
| Product generation | AOA_product-studio |
| Marketing + publishing | AOA_distribution-studio |
| Performance review | AOA_feedback-analytics |

## System Folders

### Workspaces

- `AOA_artwork-lab/` — artwork intake, interpretation, metadata, and artwork readiness
- `AOA_product-studio/` — product creation, product mapping, product records, and listing readiness
- `AOA_distribution-studio/` — social, email, website, submissions, platform notes, and audience-facing content
- `AOA_feedback-analytics/` — performance review, audience response, sales observations, and system learning

### Support Folders

- `AOA_source-intelligence/` — stable brand, voice, product, listing, workflow, and AI-generation rules
- `AOA_connecting-layer/` — placement rules, schema mapping, channel mapping, design rules, and export logic
- `AOA_datasets/` — portable structured records mirrored from or pushed to Notion
- `AOA_exports/` — raw exports, channel-ready files, and publishing/archive snapshots

## Placement Rules

Before adding or moving files, reference:

`AOA_connecting-layer/placement-rules.md`

This file defines where new rules, records, drafts, exports, submissions, platform notes, and workflow materials belong.
