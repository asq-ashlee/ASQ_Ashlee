# ASQ Ashlee — CONTEXT

Last updated: 2026-05-20
Status: active

## Purpose
ASQ Ashlee is an AI-native operational methodology and consulting ecosystem focused on helping humans and businesses think more clearly, structure workflows, and collaborate effectively with AI.

ASQ Ashlee combines:
- systems thinking
- workflow design
- operational decomposition
- AI-native architecture
- human-centered implementation
- creative and strategic business operations

The purpose is not to automate humans away.

The purpose is to:
- reduce operational friction
- improve clarity
- create reusable workflows
- support human creativity and judgment
- design sustainable AI-assisted systems

---

## Core Philosophy

- AI should sharpen thinking, not replace it
- structure increases AI usefulness
- workflows matter more than prompts
- context engineering matters more than prompt engineering
- operational clarity creates leverage
- human review is essential
- AI systems should remain interpretable
- source truth matters
- persistent intelligence should live outside chats

---

## Operational Principles

- Separate persistent intelligence from working artifacts
- Create clean workflow stages
- Keep context bounded
- Use human review between operational stages
- Build systems around real workflows
- Keep systems interpretable
- Prefer modular systems over monolithic systems
- Structure before automation
- Pipelines before agents
