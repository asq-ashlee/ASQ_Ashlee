# ASQ Ashlee

ASQ Ashlee is a public working archive for question-led AI systems, agent-readable workflows, and practical operating models for real-world creative, marketing, and career work.

This repository documents the systems I am building and testing across:

- ASQ Career OS
- ASQ Thinking Studio
- ASQ Freelance
- ASQ Creative

## Featured Systems

### ASQ Career OS

ASQ Career OS is a GPT-assisted career decision system I built to evaluate roles, pressure-test fit, choose application strategy, and structure career materials around signal, proof of work, taste, and role-specific relevance.

- Source/context folder: https://github.com/asq-ashlee/ASQ_Ashlee/tree/main/ASQ_Career_OS
- Public GPT operator: https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69f1839a24b88191b8103368b478fbc8-asq-career-os-operator

### Arts of August System

Arts of August System is a real-world creative business operating system for turning original artwork into public-facing brand, ecommerce, event, content, and workflow infrastructure.

- Source/context folder: https://github.com/asq-ashlee/ASQ_Ashlee/tree/main/ASQ_Freelance/Arts_of_August_System
- Live site: https://artsofaugust.org/

## Why this exists

Most AI work succeeds or fails at the system layer: how context is created, decisions are structured, workflows are documented, and outputs are evaluated.

ASQ Ashlee is where I document that layer.
