# ASQ Creative — CONTEXT

Last updated: 2026-05-20
Status: active

## Purpose

ASQ Creative is the experiential and community-focused workspace within the ASQ ecosystem.

This workspace explores:
- creativity
- community
- workshops
- artistic experimentation
- collaborative learning
- AI and creativity intersections
- human-centered experiences

The goal is to create environments where:
- people think together
- create together
- experiment safely
- explore AI practically
- connect systems to real life

---

## Core Activities

- workshops
- creative events
- AI roundtables
- collaborative sessions
- local partnerships
- artistic experiences
- educational experiments

---

## Experience Philosophy

Experiences should feel:
- welcoming
- thoughtful
- exploratory
- human
- creative
- non-intimidating
- collaborative

Avoid:
- excessive technical jargon
- performative futurism
- over-automation
- sterile experiences

---

## Operational Principles

- prioritize human interaction
- support curiosity
- encourage experimentation
- preserve warmth and accessibility
- create emotionally safe learning environments
