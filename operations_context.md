# Operations — CONTEXT

## Purpose

Operations is the internal business infrastructure workspace within the ASQ ecosystem.

This workspace supports:
- planning
- finances
- subscriptions
- operating systems
- administrative coordination
- sustainability

---

## Core Activities

- stack tracking
- subscription management
- financial planning
- operational review
- business administration
- workflow coordination

---

## Operational Philosophy

Operations should:
- reduce cognitive overhead
- support sustainability
- prioritize clarity
- prevent tool sprawl
- maintain operational visibility

---

## Key Principles

- tools should justify their operational cost
- systems should remain maintainable
- visibility reduces anxiety
- structure supports creativity
- workflows should remain interpretable
