# Tax Assumptions

This file stores working assumptions only.

Nothing in this file is tax advice.

## Working assumptions

- Many ASQ-related SaaS tools may be business expenses if used for business activity.
- Mixed personal/business tools may need allocation or CPA review.
- Annual subscriptions should be tracked with both actual payment date and monthly equivalent.
- Receipts and payment records should be stored somewhere secure.
- Tax categories should be treated as guesses until reviewed by a CPA/bookkeeper.

## CPA review needed

- Business entity structure
- Deductibility of mixed-use tools
- Quarterly estimated taxes
- Treatment of annual subscriptions
- Home office, equipment, education, and travel rules
