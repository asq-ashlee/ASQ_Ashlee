# Pricing Notes

Use this file to connect ASQ Ashlee pricing to actual business costs and value.

## Pricing should account for

- monthly software burn
- delivery time
- admin/project management time
- taxes
- client value
- revision/support time
- opportunity cost
- business development time
- reusable IP created through the project

## Starter offer categories

### AI Systems Audit

Diagnose where a business is losing time, context, money, or momentum, and recommend a practical AI/system workflow.

### Build Sprint

Create a working system, workflow, or AI-enabled operating layer.

### Monthly Systems Retainer

Ongoing support for business owners who need help operating, improving, and extending AI-enabled systems.

### Workshop / Training

Teach teams how to think with, use, or operationalize AI in a practical context.
