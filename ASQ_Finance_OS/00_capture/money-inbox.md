## How to use this

- I signed up for [tool] today. Trial ends [date]. It becomes [$ amount] [monthly/annually]. Cancel before [date] unless [condition].
- I paid [$ amount] for [thing] on [date]. It was for [ASQ / Arts of August / mixed / personal].
- I cancelled [tool] today. It should not renew.
- I am considering [tool]. It would cost [$ amount], but I have not signed up yet.
- I made [$ amount] from [client/product] on [date].
